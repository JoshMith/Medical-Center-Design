Priority-Based Care System

Our intelligent triage system categorizes health concerns to ensure appropriate and timely care

🚨 URGENT

Immediate Care Required

Chest pain, severe breathing difficulty, heavy bleeding, or other life-threatening symptoms require immediate medical attention.

Response: Immediate consultation
⚠️ MEDIUM PRIORITY

Timely Care Needed

Persistent fever, moderate pain, concerning rashes, or symptoms that may worsen without treatment.

Response: Appointment within 24 hours
💡 HEALTH ADVICE

Self-Care Guidance

Minor symptoms, wellness questions, or health maintenance queries that can be managed with proper guidance.

Response: AI-powered advice & monitoring
